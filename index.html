Problem Statement

Complete set of requirements for the Flight Booking System case study.

01 PS

Problem Statement-01

Get the complete details of all flights to New Delhi.

02 PS

Problem Statement-02

Get the details about all flights from Chennai to New Delhi.

03 PS

Problem Statement-03

Find only the flight numbers for passenger with pid 123 for flights to Chennai before 06/11/2020.

04 PS

Problem Statement-04

Find the passenger names for passengers who have bookings on at least one flight.

05 PS

Problem Statement-05

Find the passenger names for those who do not have any bookings in any flights.

06 PS

Problem Statement-06

Find the agency names for agencies that located in the same city as passenger with passenger id 123.

07 PS

Problem Statement-07

Get the details of flights that are scheduled on both dates 01/12/2020 and 02/12/2020 at 16:00 hours.

08 PS

Problem Statement-08

Get the details of flights that are scheduled on either of the dates 01/12/2020 or 02/12/2020 or both at 16:00 hours.

09 PS

Problem Statement-09

Find the agency names for agencies who do not have any bookings for passenger with id 123.

10 PS

Problem Statement-10

Find the details of all male passengers who are associated with Jet agency.
